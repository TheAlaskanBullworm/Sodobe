WHAT THE FLIP IS ADOBE PREMIERE PRO!?!?!?!?!?

Premiere Pro is used by filmmakers, YouTubers, videographers, designers — anyone with a story to tell, including you. Whatever you set out to do, do it with the same app Hollywood pros use. Start a vlog, go viral on YouTube, or become a go-to videographer. Import footage from any camera or phone, trim clips, add titles, and adjust audio. With intuitive tools and tons of step-by-step tutorials, you’ll be producing great work in no time. Working on your next masterpiece? Premiere Pro is the only nonlinear editor that lets you have multiple projects open while simultaneously collaborating on a single project with your team. Plus, you can lock your projects so others can’t change them. Workflows for color, graphics, audio, and immersive 360/VR take you from first edit to final credits faster than ever.
